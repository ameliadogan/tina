How Women Get Shut Out of Computer Science

Globally, women have been and continue to be an untapped resource in the computer science and engineering field. Though women make up half of the population, they only account for 20% of AP computer science exam takers (computerscience.org). Similarly, according to the National Center for Women and Information Technology, women account for only 18% of all undergraduate computer and information sciences degrees.
